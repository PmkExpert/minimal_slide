Minimal Slide is a classic puzzle game where you have to move around tiles of different shapes in order to bring one of them to the target. The levels are divided in three difficul ties with many different shapes of tiles and walls and, once beaten, you can try to complete them again with less moves until you can beat the star score, testing you ability to think of new strategies.

Every time you beat a level, you earn coins with which you can unlock colors, textures and more to customize your tiles to your liking. If you're lucky, you can even unlock s pecial rare sets with unique visuals and sounds!

Features:
- More than 100 levels divided in three difficulties
- Earn coins to spend in an in-game shop
- 2 0 colors to customize the tiles and the UI
- 30 textures, effects and sounds to customize the tiles
- 5 special sets to give an unique look to the tiles
- A stats menu with statistics about beaten levels
- Original soundtrack and sound effects
